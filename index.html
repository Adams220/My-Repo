What Is Meditation?

Meditation is a practice of using the mind and body to focus on a particular object. It is usually done individually, in a seated position and with eyes closed.
In our society today, meditation is an extremely common practice and one that has helped millions of people find inner peace in their lives.
Meditation is also practiced in Hinduism, Christianity and Islam. However, it is important to note that a person does not need to be religious to meditate.
